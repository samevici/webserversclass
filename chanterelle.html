Chanterelle is a friendly and curious cat -- but with strict boundaries! When we first found her, she was aggressive towards cats, and scary even to humans.

However, after giving her (cat-dosage) prozac, she was able to calm down, and her tolerance for petting has increased over the time she spent in foster family.

With her raspy voice and slightly spicy tendencies, we are convinced she is a demon from hell, but nevertheless we love her.

Once we learned the exact way to pet her, and we figured out how to get her into a carrier without restraining her, we did not have any problems with her anymore!

She will need to take her antidepressants all her life, as she seems to be an inherently stressed cat. Since she is an adult, her adoptions fees are 200$
