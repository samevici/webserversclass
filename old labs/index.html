Welcome to Affo-gato cat rescue!

We are a humble cat rescue that works with a network of foster families.

We focus on cats with behaviour issues who would normally have a hard time getting adopted -- or worse, would otherwise get euthanized.

All of our cats are microchipped, sterilized, and vaccinated. There will be a small adoption cost to help us cover the medical fees and keep the mission going.
